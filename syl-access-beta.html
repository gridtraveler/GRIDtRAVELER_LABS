First Launch
01
Extract & Place
Extract the ZIP. Copy the SYL folder to C:\SYL\ and create your show folder at D:\MyShow\. Place your video assets (MP4/MOV) inside D:\MyShow\.
02
Run SYL_Control.exe
Launch SYL_Control.exe first — this scans your show folder, generates the asset manifest, and starts the control server. A browser tab will open automatically with the control surface.
03
Launch the Engine
From the control surface (or directly), launch SYL.exe. The engine will initialize and your LED wall outputs will go live. Allow 30–60 seconds for full startup on first run.
04
Load Your Show
In the control surface, select your show file. Assets populate automatically. Assign clips to Wall A / B / C using the cue slots (1–4). Arm a cue, then fire — your walls respond instantly.
05
You're Live
All cues, snapshots, and wall assignments persist between sessions. The AI Director is available for natural language control via the chat panel. OSC commands are received on port 8000 from your existing show control rig.
